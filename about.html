About Me

Hello! I’m Ishan Tripathi, a BCA graduate from Varanasi 🎓. I'm originally from Jaunpur, UP. My life journey is filled with passion, plot twists, and pure desi dedication. 💪

As a child, I had one dream — to join the Indian Airforce and serve my country. 🇮🇳 But destiny had another plan, and now I’m flying through codes, not cockpits ✈️💻. Even though I couldn't join the forces, the discipline and willpower stayed with me.

I’m currently chasing the hacker's mindset — yes, I want to be a white-hat hacker, the good guy in the digital battlefield. My interest lies in Cybersecurity, Web Development, and Editing.

I’m a very curious learner, whether it’s tech, tools, or turning boring things into memes 🎨. I can’t code all day, so I spend time editing reels and being a one-man content machine. Oh and... I’m 5'11", 78kg, vegetarian, and fighting that post-dengue weight. 😤
